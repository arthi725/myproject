ARTISTIC STROKES

In art, a stroke refers to a single, deliberate mark made by a tool like a brush or pencil, defining the shape and form of an element within a piece. It's the directional movement of the tool that leaves a visible trace on the surface, and it can vary in thickness, length, and style. Artistic strokes are not just about creating lines; they can also convey emotion, texture, and the artist's unique style.
