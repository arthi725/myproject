About Painting:

Painting is a form of visual art involving the application of pigment (paint) to a surface, typically a flat one, to create an image or expression. It's a way for artists to convey ideas, emotions, and aesthetic qualities using elements like color, line, shape, and texture. Paintings can vary greatly in style, technique, and medium, from traditional oil on canvas to modern digital art.

Vincent van Gogh

Vincent van Gogh was a Dutch Post-Impressionist painter whose work, though not widely appreciated during his lifetime, is now among the most recognizable and influential in the history of Western art. He is renowned for his vibrant use of color, expressive brushstrokes, and the emotional depth conveyed in his paintings, which include iconic works like The Starry Night, Sunflowers, and numerous self-portraits.

Mauve. Vincent took painting lessons in The Hague from a cousin by marriage, the celebrated artist Anton Mauve.
